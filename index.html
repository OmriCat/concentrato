Concentrato is an Android Application to better organise your time by applying the Pomodoro Technique.

How to use Concentrato?

There are five basic steps to implementing the technique:

  1. Decide on the task to be done

    Choose one of the tasks in the list or create a new one.

  2. Start the pomodoro timer!
  3. Work on the task until the timer finishes (with an optional notification)

    Well done, you accomplished a task and your pomodoro is automatically recorded.

  4. Take a short break (3-5 minutes)
  5. Every three pomodoros take a longer break (15–30 minutes)
